ABOUT Kerry's Kiss B&B

ABOUT US

Welcome to Kerry's Kiss B&B where we take pride in making Killarney’s best kept secret a luxurious home from home for all our guests. Mostly due to the warmth of our wonderful staff and the effort they make to ensure your stay is a memorable one. We are delighted to have received many prestigious awards and accolades over the years, all of which are attributable to what Kerry's Kiss does best – excelling in the art of hospitality. We are devoted to protecting the environment and committed to the responsible use of energy, water and minerals. We take responsibility for the environmental impact of how we run our business very seriously and this ethos is instilled throughout our team. We are also committed to our community and contribute to society by sponsoring local clubs, charities and societies.

ROOMS AND FACILITIES

DINING ROOM

Enjoy breakfast in out fancy dining room. Everyone needs something nice to eat after sleeping in our beds. Breakfast is served between 8am and 10.30am each morning. Enjoy a light continental breakfast or a hearty full Irish cooked breakfast before setting out to explore the local area.

LOUNGE

Relax and contemplate in the gentle ambience of the lounge. Don’t feel cut off from news from home, we have a computer in the lounge, or bring your own laptop; there is free WiFi in all the Bedrooms, the lounge and Reception area!
