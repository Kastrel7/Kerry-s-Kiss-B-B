FAQ

Guide Dogs and Pets?

Guide Dogs are definetly allowed! All pets are allowed, although bigger animals must be kept on a leash or in a cage.

What are the restrictions on smoking?

Smoking is allowed in the communal outdoor smoking area. Smoking indoors is not permitted!

What currencies are used in Ireland

The Euro(€) is used in Ireland, depending on your bank, your credit card may convert your currency to the Euro, charges may apply
